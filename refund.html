Refund Policy

Our commitment to customer satisfaction

Last Updated: April 9, 2023

1. Overview

At VortexCheats, customer satisfaction is our priority. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which we offer refunds for our products and services. By purchasing from us, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If you experience severe technical issues that our support team cannot resolve within 24 hours of your initial report, you may be eligible for a refund. These issues must be directly related to our product and not to your hardware, software configuration, or internet connection.
  • Product Not as Described: If the product significantly differs from what was advertised on our website at the time of purchase, you may request a refund within 24 hours of purchase.
  • Double Charges: If you were accidentally charged twice for the same product, we will refund the duplicate charge immediately upon verification.
  • Unused Subscriptions: For subscription-based products, if you have not used or activated the product since purchase and request a refund within 12 hours, we may offer a full refund at our discretion.

3. Refund Ineligibility

Refunds will not be issued in the following situations:

  • User Error: We do not provide refunds for user errors, including but not limited to incorrect usage of our products, failure to follow installation instructions, or issues arising from incompatible system configurations.
  • Game Bans: We do not offer refunds if you experience a ban from a game while using our products. While our solutions are designed to minimize detection, we cannot guarantee that game developers won't implement new detection methods.
  • Change of Mind: Simply changing your mind after purchase is not grounds for a refund.
  • Expired Refund Period: Requests made after the 24-hour eligibility period has expired.
  • Product Already Used: If you have already used the product for its intended purpose, even if only briefly.
  • Violation of Terms: If you have violated our Terms of Service in any way.
  • Payment Disputes: If you have initiated a chargeback or payment dispute without first attempting to resolve the issue with our support team.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team through our Discord server or by emailing support@vortexcheats.com within 24 hours of your purchase.
  2. Provide your order number, the email address used for purchase, and a detailed explanation of the issue you're experiencing.
  3. Our support team will first attempt to resolve any technical issues. Please cooperate with troubleshooting efforts.
  4. If the issue cannot be resolved and your refund request meets our eligibility criteria, we will process your refund within 3-5 business days.

5. Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. Processing times may vary depending on your payment provider:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Cryptocurrency: 1-3 business days (may vary based on blockchain congestion)

6. Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

  • If you purchased a bundle and wish to return only part of it
  • If you have used a subscription service for a significant portion of its term
  • If we determine that a full refund is not warranted but wish to offer partial compensation

The amount of any partial refund will be determined at our discretion based on the specific circumstances.

7. Special Offers and Promotions

Products purchased during special promotions, discounts, or sales may have modified refund terms, which will be clearly communicated at the time of purchase. In general, heavily discounted items may not be eligible for refunds unless they are completely non-functional.

8. Subscription Cancellations

Cancellation of a subscription is different from requesting a refund:

  • You may cancel a subscription at any time through your account settings.
  • Cancellation will stop future billing cycles but will not provide a refund for the current billing period.
  • You will continue to have access to the subscription benefits until the end of the current billing period.

9. Account Status After Refund

Upon refund approval:

  • Your access to the refunded product will be immediately revoked.
  • Any hardware ID linked to the license will be removed from our system.
  • If you received a refund for a subscription, your subscription will be canceled.

10. Modifications to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. It is your responsibility to review our Refund Policy periodically for changes. Your continued use of our services following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
